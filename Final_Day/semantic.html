What is Semantic HTML?

Semantic means meaning. So, Semantic HTML means using HTML tags that clearly describe what the content is, not just how it looks. Think of it this way: Instead of saying: "This is just a box." You say: "This is the website header." or "This is the navigation menu." or "This is the footer." The browser, search engines, and screen readers all understand the purpose of each section.
