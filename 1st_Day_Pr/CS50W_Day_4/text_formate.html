HTML Text Formatting Tags - Real Life Examples

1. <b> - Bold (Only makes text bold)

Our Summer Sale starts today!

2. <strong> - Important Text

Warning: Do not share your password with anyone.

3. <i> - Italic

The book Harry Potter is very popular.

4. <em> - Emphasized Text

You must submit the assignment before Friday.

5. <u> - Underlined Text

Please read the instructions carefully.

6. <mark> - Highlighted Text

Your exam is on 25 July.

7. <small> - Small Text

Price: $99.99

*Terms and conditions apply.

8. <del> - Deleted Text

Old Price: $150

9. <ins> - Inserted Text

New Price: $99

10. <sub> - Subscript

Water Formula: H2O

11. <sup> - Superscript

Math Formula: x2 + y2
